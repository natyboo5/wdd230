Design Principles Document

Natalia Paredes

Alignment

Federal Loan IDR Plans
myfedloan.org

The media objects are precisely aligned to the left and aid in the visualization of the different steps of the "IDR life cycle". This design is clean and it keeps the content well organized and structured.

Repetition

Zumba
zumba.com

This page has 3 different elements that offer the same: "a Zumba class". It is clear that the company wants to make the user aware of what is the main purpose of the page. Although there is more content to explore, the repetition of the class search conveys that message.

Proximity

Instagram
instagram.com

The stories "bubbles" on the top part of the page are a good example of proximity. All those little pictures are grouped together to represent one type of content, and located at the top, they separate themselves from the rest of the page creating a focal point. They enhance the user's experience by being easy to access.
